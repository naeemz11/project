How To Cook Ramen: instructions

  1. Turn on the stove and heat up the sesame oil in a pan over medium low heat
  2. Add garlic and ginger. Stir the ingredients for 2 minutes or until it softens
  3. Add the broth into the pan
  4. Add mushrooms and lower the heat to simmer for 10 minutes or until the mushroom softens
  5. Add the noodles and let it simmer for another 5 minutes or until the noodles softens
  6. Add scallions and stir it to mix with the noodles
  7. Turn off the heat and then add carrots. Stir to mix
  8. Cut the soft boiled eggs in half and add on top of the noodles
  9. Add any additional topping for seasoning: chili oil, sesame oil and salt
  10. Lastly, add chopped scallions and carrots for garnish
